BoquilaHUB

Cross-platform tool for running computer vision models on wildlife data. On your own hardware, no cloud required.

Why BoquilaHUB?

  • Local AI Deployment (No Cloud needed)
  • Configurable Inference
  • Intuitive, Simple User Interface
  • Analyze images, video, or real-time camera feed
